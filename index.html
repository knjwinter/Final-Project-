Learn More About Us

Change the way you eat, change the way you live!

  • The purpose of the FPT is to bring awareness to the food deserts happening in urban cities, specifically thoes in low income communities which historitically dont have access to fresh foods.
  • We all know your access to nutritious food effects your health, how well you preform in school as well as ties into your social economic background.
  • Changing the way black/brown people eat(thoes who are typically in low income/urban environments) can change everything from education, to job access.
